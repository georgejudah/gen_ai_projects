Agents

What are they?
Software entities that can autonomously perform tasks that are goal oriented and are task specific.

They are designed to work as a part of agent framework to solve complex problems with
limited human investment.

- Memory/Persistance.
- Decision Making/ orchestration
- Planning capabilities
- Use of tools; potentially connecting to databases or the internet.


What we are going to accomplish ?
Image generation, Create agents to generate sounds and image for our store, Make an agent framework - teach our AI assistant to speak
and draw.
